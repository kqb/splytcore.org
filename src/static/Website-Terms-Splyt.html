2. Use of Cookies
2.1 This website uses cookies to better the users experience while visiting the website.

2.2 Cookies are small files saved to the user's computers hard drive that track, save and store

website.
2.3 Users are advised that if they wish to deny the use and saving of cookies from this

use it. This software is provided by Google Analytics which uses cookies to track visitor

information http://www.google.com/privacy.html
